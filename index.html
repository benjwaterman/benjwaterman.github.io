Water Simulation

This is what I built for my final year project and the basis of my dissertation; a flood management game with semi-realistic water. You are tasked with protecting buildings from the water that threatens to burst through the river and flood the city. By strategically placing defences and managing your money, it is your job to keep the water at bay.
