    Water Simulation

    This is what I built for my final year project and the basis of my dissertation; a flood management game with semi-realistic water. You are tasked with protecting buildings from the water that threatens to burst through the river and flood the city. By strategically placing defences and managing your money, it is your job to keep the water at bay.

    Mobile App

    • Java
    • Android

    This mobile app was developed using Android Studio, with its main aim being to integrate REST APIs. By connecting to Twitch.tv's API, it allows for viewing of the top streamed games and channels. Favourites can also be added, as well as the ability to locate game stores based on the user's location.

    Monsters and Magic

    • Unity
    • C#

    One of my biggest personal projects, I spent Summer of 2016 developing and coding this game. Features include a dialogue system, a quest system and a combat system. Your job as an apprentice mage is to close the portals and defeat the enemies that are threatening to destroy your village.

    Chucky Egg

    • OpenGL
    • C++
    • Networking

    The aim of this project was to create a clone of the classic game Chucky Egg using OpenGL/SDL. Animations, collisions, sound triggers and multiplayer were all custom coded with help from various open source libraries. The local network multiplayer allows two players to play together from seperate machines.

    Slimes n Switches

    • Unity
    • C#

    The task was to design and program a game with its core mechanic being based on Boolean logic. In this game, the player takes control of two slimes to complete various puzzles by switching between them at will. Using a combination of precision platforming and quick reflexes, you must complete each level as quickly as possible by jumping on buttons, shooting levers and avoiding spikes.
